Speeds for the Lathe and Mill

Given the SFM, what RPM should be used


This calculator returns the RPM (revolutions per minute) for a given SFM (surface feet per minute) entered. There is a simple formula for these calculations:

RPM = 3.82 × SFM Diameter

To use the calculator simply enter the SFM desired and the diameter of your part (for lathe work) or the diameter of your milling cutter (for milling). The following table displays some common SFM recommendations.

Material Recommended SFM (HSS) Recommended SFM (Carbide)
Mild steel 90 180
Tool steel 50 100
Aluminum & brass 150 300
Bronze 100 200
Stainless steel 40 80
