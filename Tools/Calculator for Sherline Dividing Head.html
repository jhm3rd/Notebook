Gear Cutting Calculator for the Sherline Dividing Head

The Sherline dividing head uses rack offsets to rotate the dividing head precise amounts. Using this calculator is essential to avoid accumulating error in the successive rotations of the head and the workpiece.

The calculator returns a list of offsets for the dividing head rack based on the DP and number of teeth of the desired gear. The following table shows the correct gear cutter based on the number of gear teeth.

Number of Teeth Cutter Number
135 to Rack 1
55 to 134 2
35 to 54 3
26 to 34 4
21 to 25 5
17 to 20 6
14 to 16 7
12 to 13 8
