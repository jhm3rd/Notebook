Schwarzchild Radius

The Schwarzchild radius is the radius defining the event horizon of a black hole. It can be calculated for any given mass.

The formula for calculating the Schwarzchild radius, RS , in meters is as follows:

R S = 2 G M c 2

Where M is in kilograms, c is in meters/second, and G is in meters3/kg/seconds2.
