Molecular Weight Calculator

Calculate Molecular Weights for Entered Formulas


This calculator is useful for determining the molecular weight for any entered formula. The formula can be entered from the keyboard or from the periodic table and its associated number buttons. After entering the formula by either or both methods click the "Calculate Molecular Weight" button to get the molecular weight.

























The elemental weights are in the MolWtCalc.js file (lines 217 - 400) and can be modified as needed.
